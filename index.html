Article Title

The drone industry has recently been subject to major advances in hardware and software elements as well as a large increase in UAV enthusiasts and hobbyist communities. Yet, drones are not only used for recreational or entertainment purposes. Drones are gradually being integrated into everyday life, replacing common everyday systems and introducing a variety of new concepts for improvement.
