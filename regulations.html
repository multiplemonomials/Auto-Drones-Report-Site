Drone Regulations

With the recent advent of autonomous drones as a product for the masses, governments have been struggling to create rules for how they can and can't be used.

Why Regulate

In general, people view government regulations as a giant wet blanket on anything they touch. However, in the case of drones, there have been several times, both intentional and accidental, when a drone caused large-scale mischeif or danger.
